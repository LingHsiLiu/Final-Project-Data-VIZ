In Pie Charts, it shows total unemployed population and unemployment rate for US States from 2011 to 2015. The blue color is employment rate and the red color is unemployment rate. The total US unemployment rate in 2011 is 9%. In 2015, it is droped down to 5.3%. In pie charts, we will easily to know unemployment rate is going down and the economic became better in United States.

Narrative Structure
The narrative structure is an interactive slide show.
The first slide is Map chart, the second slide is bar chart and the third slide is pie chart.
Click on the arrow on the top or bottom to next slideshow.

Scenes
There are a total 3 scenes in this slideshow.
The 1 slide is map chart. It shows unemployment rate for each state. And, I use different colors to show different rate.
The 2 slide is bar chart. It shows unemployment rate by bar chart.
The 3 slide is pie chart. It shows total US unemployment rate.

Parameters
In the Map chart and bar chart, Parameters is unemployment rateParameters for each state.
In the pie chart, I use unemployment population to run how many unemployment rate for all United States.

Annotations
Annotation 1: When you move to map, the color will change and show the state name and unemployment rate for each year.
Annotation 2: When you click vega editor of each bar chart, it will shows unemployment rate for states and show the state name.
Annotation 3: When you move to pie chart, it shows how many people are unemployed and employed and show the rate.

Triggers
Triggers 1 : In 1 slide, we can click year to see different unemployment rate in different year.
Triggers 2 : In each slide, we can click arrow botton of top and bottom to go next slide or previous slide.
